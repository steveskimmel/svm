This app is based on the Sales Velocity Model from the Altify Group

It is enhanced from an adaptation of the Forrester Benchmark metrics to indicate the improvement ranges on your variables by using Salesforce Automation.

Range Improvements by using Salesforce Automation:

  • # Deals: 0% - 5%
  • Deal Size: 10% - 20%
  • % Win Rate: 8% - 10%
  • Sales Cycle: 12% - 20%
